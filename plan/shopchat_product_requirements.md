# ShopChat - Conversational Commerce Platform

## Product Overview
ShopChat flips the traditional e-commerce model by making chat the primary buying experience. It's a "Direct Seller-Buyer Messaging" platform where negotiation, customization, and transactions happen within the conversation.

## Core Flow
1. **Browse:** Buyer discovers products.
2. **Inquire:** Buyer clicks "Ask Seller" to start a chat.
3. **Converse:** Negotiation, customization, and Q&A happen in real-time.
4. **Transact:** Seller sends a payment link or buyer clicks "Buy Now" inside the chat.
5. **Fulfill:** Tracking updates and reviews stay in the same chat thread.

## Business Model
- **Free Tier:** 10 products, unlimited chats, 5% fee.
- **Paid Tier ($19/mo):** Unlimited products, 2% fee, auto-responses, analytics.

## Design Requirements
- **Vibe:** Enterprise-grade, clean, trustworthy, and modern.
- **Modes:** Light and Dark mode support.
- **Responsiveness:** Desktop, Tablet, and Mobile views.
